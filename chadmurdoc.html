About Chad Murdock

Chad Murdock is a film director who has worked on several films and music videos for high-profile brands such as Nike, Facebook, Proenza Schouler, and Valentino [^1^][1]. He is also the director of the film "Fathers and Sons" (2019), which has received global recognition and can still be found on his Vimeo channel [^1^][1].
